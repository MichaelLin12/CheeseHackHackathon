Goods ditribution tracker 

With the financial difficulties and hardships caused by the pandemic, the goventment initiated aid programs in partnership together with local organizations and volunteers in the area. 

Our Webapp tracks the state-aided distributions of food, clothes, home accessories, and other supplies   to the local community.

[Present the app]
Organizations are able to add their goods distribution schedule, location, and types of goods. 
Users view and be notified of the goods distributions in their region.
